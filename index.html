Muhurtha Kaal Undruthal Event Schedule

November 22 - November 28

Muhurtha Kaal Undruthal

Friday, Nov 22

Kalyanam Soluthal - Proclamation of Marriage

Saturday, Nov 23

Following Muhurtha Kaal Undruthal, the immediate family of the bride and groom formally invite the grandparents, aunts, and uncles to the wedding.

Kumbuduthal - Offering to God and Ancestors

Tuesday, Nov 26

It is customary to start by praying to God and ancestors before any good event...

Kudi Aaki Unuthal - Gathering

Tuesday, Nov 26

Two days before the wedding, the extended family starts gathering in the wedding house and the formal feast festivities kick off.

Thumbu Pidithal

Wednesday, Nov 27

The day before the wedding, two married women draw a temple pattern on the wall using thread and rice batter...

Aarasanikal Naduthal - Planting of the King's Flag

Wednesday, Nov 27

Over the course of time, as the king could no longer personally be there, the Aarasanikal (flag stand) and flag made of branches are placed to represent his presence.

Thirupootuthal - Tying of the Knot

Thursday, Nov 28, 10 AM

The bride and groom are brought to the manai by the mamakarangal (maternal uncles). The groom ties the Kaluthiru with three knots, officially uniting the couple...

Isaipidimanam - Formal Marriage Agreement

Thursday, Nov 28, 11:30 AM

The fathers of the bride and groom register the wedding in the temple book, continuing a longstanding Chettiar tradition.
